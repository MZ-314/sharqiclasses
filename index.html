What is Advanced Mathematics? Advanced Mathematics is a separate mathematics paper offered by the SEBA (Assam State Board of Secondary Education) to Class 9 & 10 students — distinct from the standard Mathematics paper. It covers additional topics and is only available to SEBA students. If you're on SEBA board and take Advanced Maths, I can help with both papers.
